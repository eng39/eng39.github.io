Avacodo Toast

This is the best recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • olive oil
  • avacodo
  • bacon bits
  • basil
  • cherry tomatoes
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. wash cherry tomatoes and basil
  2. cut bread, cherry tomatoes, and avacodo
  3. spread avacodo on bread
  4. place cherry tomatoes
  5. prinkle bacon bits, salt and pepper
  6. poor over olive oil
